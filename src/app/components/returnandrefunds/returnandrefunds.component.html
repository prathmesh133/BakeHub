Return & Refund Policy

Please Read Carefully Before Placing Your Order

Our goal is to ensure your complete satisfaction with every order.
Since cakes are perishable and custom-made, please review our return and refund terms below:

  • No returns are accepted for perishable items such as cakes.
  • Refunds will be issued only in the following cases:
    • Damaged cake received at the time of delivery
    • Wrong item delivered that doesn't match the order
    • Order not delivered at all
  • Time limit for raising complaints:
    Any issues must be reported within 1 hour of delivery with photo evidence.
  • How to claim a refund:
    Contact our support team by phone or email and share the order ID, complaint details, and supporting photo.
  • No refunds will be provided in case of:
    • Late deliveries due to traffic, weather, or unforeseen external conditions
    • Incorrect address or unavailability of recipient at the delivery location
